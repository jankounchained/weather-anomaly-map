violin: draw both halves via `violinShape()` (curve path or rug), two per-half
mean ticks, and the preserved actual-value diamond — all on the single shared
`TrendYAxisColumn` scale. Verify `App.tsx` / `TrendRow.tsx` flow the new
two-sample `TrendDayResult` through unchanged (wiring only; type-flow, not
structural rewrite).

Purpose: this is the phase's user-visible payoff — the tile a reader looks at.
The prior-left / recent-right halves plus the mean-tick gap surface the
climate-shift signal; the rug fallback keeps thin halves honest; the shared axis
keeps all 7 days comparable.

diamond + axis, and confirmed wiring.
</objective>
