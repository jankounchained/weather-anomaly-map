## Phase Goal (user story)

**As a** person checking a location's weather, **I want to** see each of the last 7 days plotted against that day's own 30-year historical distribution — with every year's values visible, the average emphasized, and a clear key for what each mark means — **so that** I can tell at a glance how unusual the whole recent week has been, not just today.

> Plan 06 is the re-verification gate for the gap-closure fixes in Plan 05. It mirrors the 03-04 human-verify checkpoint exactly (the phase's built-in end-to-end UAT), but its job is to confirm the two blocking defects from 03-04 are actually resolved against live data: the leftmost tile matches its siblings, and the new legend makes the marks legible without hovering. This is the authoritative pass/fail gate per 03-VERIFICATION.md's own "missing" items for both gaps — it is a distinct blocking checkpoint, not folded into the code-fix plan.

<objective>

Also re-confirm the full VIZ-01 / VIZ-02 / ROBU-01 experience shipped in Plans 01-05 still holds end-to-end (shared scale, today rightmost, distinct marks, graceful no-data path).

Purpose: The phase's core value is visual interpretability ("easy to interpret at a glance"). Automated tests prove the props render and the math is correct; only a human can certify the row now reads as intended and the legend resolves the legibility bar the previous checkpoint failed on.

Output: Human approval (Phase 3 can close), or a fresh punch-list to route into another gap-closure cycle. No source files are modified by this plan.
