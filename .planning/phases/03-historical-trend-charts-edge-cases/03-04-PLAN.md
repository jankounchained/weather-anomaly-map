## Phase Goal (user story)

**As a** person checking a location's weather, **I want to** see each of the last 7 days plotted against that day's own 30-year historical distribution — with every year's values visible and the average emphasized — plus a clear message when a day lacks enough history, **so that** I can tell at a glance how unusual the whole recent week has been, not just today.

> Plan 04 is the end-to-end sign-off. Everything shipped in Plans 01-03 is unit- and build-verified, but the whole phase is fundamentally a visual "at a glance" experience — the readability of 7 dot/strip charts at 88px, the emphasis of the mean over the spread, and the shared-scale comparability can only be confirmed by a human looking at it against live data. This checkpoint mirrors Phase 2's 02-03 human-verify pattern.

<objective>
Human-verify the complete 7-day trend row end-to-end against live Open-Meteo data (VIZ-01, VIZ-02, ROBU-01): confirm the small-multiples row reads as a scannable week, the distribution + emphasized mean + distinct actual marker are legible, the shared y-scale makes days comparable, and the "Not enough data" placeholder + stricter today-anomaly gate behave gracefully.

Purpose: The phase's core value is visual interpretability ("easy to interpret at a glance"). Automated tests prove the props render and the math is correct; only a human can confirm the charts are actually readable and the emphasis reads correctly.

Output: Human approval (or a punch-list of visual issues to route into gap closure). No source files are modified by this plan.
</objective>
